This program contains a list of all the US presidents with their first name, last name, and year elected.
There are 3 options to sort the presidents: either by first name, last name, or year elected.
Enter -l to sort alphabetically by last name.
Enter -f to sort alphabetically by first name.
Enter -y to sort by year elected from earliest to latest.
